Slow Cooker Chicken and Veggies

Description

I'm sensing a chicken theme here.

Another super simple (but super delicious) meal from Sarah. This is a recipe I used often in the early years of my marriage. Fun fact: I've always used chicken breasts instead of chicken tenderloins.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds boneless skinless chicken tenderloins (or chicken breasts)
  • 1 pound bag baby carrots
  • 1 ½ pounds Yukon gold potatoes (cut into wedges)
  • ½ cup salted butter (melted)
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic (or one teaspoon garlic powder)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme leaves (not ground)
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley

Steps

  1. Add the chicken down in the middle of the slow cooker. Add the potatoes on one side and the carrots on the other side.
  2. In a small bowl mix together the butter, garlic, salt, pepper, thyme and parsley.
  3. Pour the butter mixture over the chicken and veggies.
  4. Cover and cook on HIGH for 4 hours or low for 6-8 hours.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
