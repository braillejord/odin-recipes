Cheesy Sausage Breakfast Casserole

Description

I had never made a breakfast casserole until this one. I'd never actually browned sausage before, either. The fact that there's only four ingredients sold me on it immediately. I even added peppers to this recipe, which is saying a lot.

Ingredients

  • 1 can crescent rolls
  • 1 pound mild breakfast sausage
  • 6 eggs beaten
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Brown sausage in skillet, drain excess grease.
  3. Spray 9x13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. Spread crescent rolls in bottom of dish.
  4. Top with sausage, then pour eggs over sausage. Generously top with cheese.
  5. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
